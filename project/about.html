we are a team

There’s a power in a group of people who work together toward a shared goal. A properly managed team boosts strengths and brings out the best in each member. There’s no better way to achieve more than by working together.

we are international

We make conscious efforts to ensure that we are always thinking bigger than Singapore. The importance of global collaboration among artists and office teams is imperative. There’s a lot we can share and learn from each other.

we are colour-minded

Different hues can provoke a range of emotions, associations, and responses that affect how your mural is perceived. Color choices can make or break a design. In fact, research has shown that color can increase brand recognition.

clients influence our ideas

What's the big picture? What's going on in the market? We're all ears, tell us more. Visual intuition is shaped by our time and the culture we live in. Art is the voice that our culture speaks, the indicator of how our society manifests itself.

we like elephants

Who doesn't? Like us, elephants can get sunburned, so they toss sand over their bodies to protect themselves. They also stop their offspring from getting burnt by standing over them to cast a shadow.
Isn't that neat?

... and sometimes we don't want to be in work either

And that's ok. We all have different things outside of work we don't want to miss, which is why MC provides flexible working hours, to be agreed both by clients and our staff at the beginning of each project.
